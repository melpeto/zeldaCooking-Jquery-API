Link's Kitchen

A Cooking Resource for Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

In Breath of the Wild, certain items can be added as ingredients to a Cooking Pot. Each ingredient carries specific effects that are applied to the food when it is cooked.

Choose your desired effect from the list below to see all the ingredients which will lend that effect to your dish when cooked in the cooking pot.

Items with no special effect will still increase hearts when eaten or cooked

Effects are granted to Link when the food is consumed, and have a time limit. These timed effects cannot be stacked - when there is one effect active, eating a food with a different, or even the same effect, will cancel the previously active effect.

Hearty and Enduring effects don't stack either, but if multiples of these dishes are consumed, instead of cancelling the previous effect, the strongest one will remain active
